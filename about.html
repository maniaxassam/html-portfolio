About

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is John and I'm a professional guitar player with over 10 years of experience. Here are some of my accomplishments:

  • Graduated from the Berklee College of Music with a degree in Guitar Performance.
  • Performed at numerous music festivals and events, including the NAMM Show, SXSW, and the Montreal Jazz Festival.
  • Released two albums as a solo artist, both of which received critical acclaim and were featured on several music blogs and websites.
  • Worked as a session musician for several well-known artists, including John Mayer and Taylor Swift.
  • Currently a member of the band "The Black Keys", with whom I have toured extensively and recorded two albums.

    • In addition to my performance work, I'm also an experienced guitar teacher. Here are some highlights of my teaching career:

  • Taught guitar lessons at the Berklee College of Music for 3 years.
  • Developed and taught a guitar curriculum for a music school in Los Angeles.
  • Created a series of instructional guitar videos that have been viewed by thousands of students worldwide.
  • Offer private guitar lessons both in-person and online, catering to students of all levels and ages.

    • Thank you for visiting my portfolio! If you're interested in learning more about my work as a musician or guitar teacher, please don't hesitate to get in touch.
